### 征兵
与征兵、入伍有关的单词主要包括但不限于以下几个：
- Recruit：指招募新兵
- Conscript：征召入伍，made to join
- Draft：也指征兵，尤其是在紧急情况下或为了特定任务而进行的征兵
- Enlist：指自愿报名参军
- Induct：指正式吸收某人为成员，尤指征召入伍成为义务兵。这个单词强调了一个从非成员到成员的正式转变过程。

### 军事行动
- manoeuvre 军事演习

- Ambush 伏击
- annex 强占，并吞，兼并

  - The country decided to wage war against its enemies.（该国决定向敌人发动战争。）
- 包围
  - They surrounded the enemy.
  - They encircled the enemy.

### 进攻

- **attack** /əˈtæk/ 进攻，攻击 **常用度：10**
  The army launched a surprise **attack** at dawn.
- **assault** /əˈsɔːlt/ 猛攻，突袭 **常用度：8**
  The enemy began a heavy **assault** on the western front.
- **offensive** /əˈfensɪv/ 进攻，攻势 **常用度：7**
  They planned a major **offensive** to regain lost territory.
- **strike** /straɪk/ 打击，攻击（军事/突然袭击）**常用度：7**
  The air force carried out a precision **strike** on the target.
- **charge** /tʃɑːrdʒ/ 冲锋，进攻（尤指战斗中）**常用度：6**
  The soldiers **charged** toward the enemy's position.
- **raid** /reɪd/ 袭击，突袭（常指小规模突袭）**常用度：6**
  The commandos launched a night-time **raid** on the base.
- **invade** /ɪnˈveɪd/ 入侵，进攻（通常指大规模军事行为）**常用度：8**
  The country was **invaded** by enemy forces in the early hours.
- **advance** /ədˈvæns/ 推进，前进（含进攻意味）**常用度：7**
  The troops began their **advance** into enemy territory.
- **onslaught** /ˈɒn.slɔːt/ 猛攻，突击 **常用度：5**
  The town was not prepared for such a fierce **onslaught**.
- **incursion** /ɪnˈkɜːʒən/ 突袭，侵犯（多指突然或短暂的进攻）**常用度：4**
  The border guards repelled an enemy **incursion** last night.
